# Popover

### Popovers are floating components used to display, upon interaction, information and actions related to the content on the screen.

---
<br />

## When to use:
- To create dropdowns;
- To show additional information or actions on a certain interface element;

<br />

## When not to use:
- As a replacement for Tooltips, that is, having only small portions of text as content;
- As a substitute for modals;
- To show critical information for the success of a user action;
