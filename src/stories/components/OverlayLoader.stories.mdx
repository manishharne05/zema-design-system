# OverlayLoader

### OverlayLoader is used to indicate the loading of internal page components, such as cards and graphics.
---
<br />

## When to use:
- To indicate the loading of page elements that do not yet have a skeleton implemented.

<br />

## When not to use:
- Do not use OverlayLoader to load pages. In these cases, choose Spinner.
- Do not use OverlayLoader to indicate loading of components that have skeletons implemented
