# Pagination

### Pagination are status indicators used to make important metadata evident.
---
<br />

## When to use:
- It is necessary to show statuses associated with business logic or user actions.
- The content to be shown is *readonly*.
- You need to categorize something.


<br />

## When not to use:
- The content that Pagination represents can be set or removed by the user.
- Clicking on the component needs to perform an action or functionality.
