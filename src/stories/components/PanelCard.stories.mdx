# PanelCard

### PanelCards are components used to group information into containers with a minimal structure consisting of title, subtitle, and content.
---
<br />

## When to use:
- Create pages and dashboards with cars in a grid.
- The actions slot must group icons or buttons that apply changes to all information contained in the PanelCard.

<br />

## When not to use:
- It is necessary to group elements outside the basic structure: title + subtitle + content.
- It is the only component of its type on the page, occupying the entire available width.
