# GaugeChart

### The Gauge Chart is designed to show progress towards a specific goal.
---
<br />

## When to use:
- Use "Gauge charts" when you need to monitor and visually communicate the status of key metrics or performance indicators;
- Use "Gauge charts" to communicate the overall state of a single metric in a quick and visually impactful way.

<br />

## When not to use:
- Avoid using "Gauge charts" to represent complex or multidimensional data sets. This type of chart is most effective when displaying a single metric against a scale.
