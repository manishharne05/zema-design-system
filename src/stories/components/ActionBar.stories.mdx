# ActionBar

### ActionBars are toolbars that can display actions and information to the user.
---
<br />

## When to use:
- To group actions that the user can take on items in a list or table.
- There is no space in menus and other places to display actions and information.

<br />

## When not to use:
- To view system statuses.
- In floating mode, the ActionBar permanently hides information on the screen.
