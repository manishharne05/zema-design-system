# CalloutCard
### CalloutCards are components used to provide the user with useful information, 
such as tutorials, shortcuts or tips.
---
<br />

## When to use:
- It is necessary to show shortcuts, tips or tutorials in a non-intrusive and 
visually striking way.

<br />

## When not to use:
- It is necessary to show messages related to changes or special conditions that 
occur in a component or page. For these cases, the use of Alert is recommended.

<br />
